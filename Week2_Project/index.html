The Great Hotels of the Strip

These hotels are part of the great and wonderful playground known as the Las Vegas Strip.

Caesars Palace

Caesars Palace has music for a wide variety of taste including that sexy southern twang of Shania Twain, and the soothing rhythm of Elton Johns calming voice. Those are just two of Caesar's Palaces headliners, they have many more great acts. One of the worst acts (in my opinion) is Gorden Ramsay's kitchen. I am not a fan of his horrible attitude towards those he works with but I really can't give you an honest opinion about the quality of his food..

Circus Circus

Circus Circus is the best place in Las Vegas for families. Gambling and shows for the adults and games and shows for the kids. Something for everybody in the family, and of course the room rates are very reasonable. The last time we stayed the room was $21 a night, that means more money in your pocket for gambling..actually for the kids to play their games which tend to be more expensive.

The Bellagio

The Bellagio Hotel and Casino is most famous for its great water show. You can't get a musical dance of water quite like that anywhere else. It is spectacular, even if you don't stay at the Bellagio it is a definite must see while on your Vegas excursion. Of course the water show isn't everything there is the Bellagio has great shows like the famous "O" and beautiful rooms just waiting for you.

Find some of the best cuisine in the US at these great restaurants.

The Beauty of Vegas

Built in a landscape of dry, hot and unfavorable conditions is a wonderland of lights, bells and beauty. If you have ever been to Disneyland you will remember that feeling you had when you went your very first time and every time after that. Your body is just filled with delight and happiness, well Las Vegas gives you that same feeling in an adult way. It's as if you stepped into a different world, a fantasy land that makes you forget about reality while you are there. That could be good and bad if you have trouble with self-control (that is the key to surviving Vegas by the way).

The best time to get to the Vegas Strip is at night when all the lights are lighting up the streets and atmosphere with a beauty that you just don't see every day. The city really doesn't sleep, it is in constant motion trying to keep you engaged and in a trance. Where else can you get great homemade tacos at 3 in the morning, and not just any tacos these tacos where great and just around the corner from Circus Circus. One of the great things about the Vegas Strip is the fact that you can see some of the old hotels and casinos that started the migration and expansion of the gambling scene.

Some may call it the devils playground, well if hell is anything like Vegas you can sign me up! Las Vegas is just like any other form of temptation, you must realize what is right and what is pushing the limits of morality (which is of course different for everyone)
